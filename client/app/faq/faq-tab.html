Yes, there is a 15 minute grace period. If you are more than 15 minutes late your appointment will have to be rescheduled depending on the service.

What is the purpose of paying a deposit?

A $20 deposit is required in order to book an appointment. The deposit goes toward the cost of your service and secures your appointment slot.
All deposits are non-refundable, but may be used for one reschedule if I am notified 48 hours in advance. If you are a no call no show or late to your appointment, you will lose your deposit.

How long does an appointment take?

The duration of an appointment depends on the service. Added designs and embellishments can easily add another hour on top of your initial service. On average it takes about 2-3 hours per client at that point.

Why am I being charged more for a fill compared to your regular clients?

I prefer not to work over other nail tech’s acrylic work due to product and technique differences. It becomes a little more work on my end having to reshape/rebalance a set to my style. My number one concern is not knowing if you have MMA on your nails. I refuse to work over it due to its hazardous properties. Another concern is not knowing how long you have actually had the previous set, as lifting could have occurred making you prone to fungus and bacteria underneath your nail. I usually do an assessment of your previous set before making an exception.

What is MMA?

Methyl Methacrylate is an ingredient that can be found in illegal monomer liquids. In the early 1970’s, the Food and Drug Administration received numerous complaints of personal injuries associated with the use of acrylic monomer formulated with MMA. Unfortunately, MMA is still being sold by the gallon illegally in the states and is very poisonous to humans. The reports included serious nail damage or loss, contact dermatitis, organ damage from long term use, soreness and infection due to breaks caused by rigidly adhered acrylic.

Do your products contain MMA?

No I am proud to say that I use the Tammy Taylor acrylic system, which does not contain the MMA chemical. My clients are more than welcome to ask to see my product containers and read the ingredients.

Do you re-use buffers and files?

I NEVER re-use my buffers or files. All files, disposable drill bits, and buffers are disposed after each client. All bare drill bits and general implements are sanitized with antibacterial soap, disinfected with hospital grade EPA, and sterilized in an autoclave after each client.
