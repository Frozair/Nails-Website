About

Angel has been a licensed manicurist since 2010 and has personally observed the nail industry constantly reinventing itself to keep up with the latest fashion trends. Within the past five years, Angel has been offering quality service to the public. There is nothing more important than assuring a safe and clean environment for the well-being of your clients. Having worked for St. David’s Medical Center for six years, Angel prides herself in practicing immaculate sanitary procedures that she is adamant about.

The revolutionary new Nail Art is in high demand internationally and has now arrived here in Austin! Angel feels that as a manicurist, it is essential to keep up with what people want. As a booming city, Austin is filled with diversity and fashion trends from all over the world. Austin has jumped on the bandwagon when it comes to new trendy Nail Art. There is a plethora of designs to choose from. From the more conservative traditional manicure to the designs that will “Keep Austin Weird”, Angel loves the challenge!
